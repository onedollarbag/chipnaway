Use the map to look up nearby programs, clinics, and community resources that can help with healthy weight‑loss and heart‑health.

Search the map

This map uses free OpenStreetMap data. You can search for terms like “weight loss clinic”, “dietitian”, or “fitness center”, then zoom in to see options near you.

Tips for choosing safe support

  • Look for licensed medical providers or registered dietitians when possible.
  • Be cautious of programs that promise extremely fast results or require large upfront fees.
  • Ask how a program supports awareness, proper diet, and sustainable exercise.
  • Talk with your doctor before making major changes to your diet, medications, or exercise.
